# insafismath.com

A personal site for **Insaf Ismath** — AI/ML Engineer & Researcher based in Abu Dhabi.

The goal: a comprehensive profile I can link from my CV so recruiters and collaborators can see who I am, what I've built, what I've published, and what I care about — all in one place.

It should cover:

- **About me** — who I am, my background, my story
- **Projects** — enterprise AI work, research projects, independent builds
- **Research publications** — papers with abstracts and PDFs
- **Resources** — talks, repos, models, writing
- **Hobbies** — the human side (padel, karting, running, football)

Build with **Next.js** on **Vercel**, deploy at `insafismath.com`.
