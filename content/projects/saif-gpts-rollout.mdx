## Why Playwright

The platform's provisioning surface was a UI, not an API. Browser automation was the
honest answer: deterministic flows, screenshots for audit, and the ability to fail
loudly when an upstream change broke the path.

## Where the leverage came from

The matching layer - role × function × hierarchy → GPT - turned a one-by-one
assignment problem into a query. Once that mapping was clean, provisioning thousands
of GPTs was a loop, not a project.
