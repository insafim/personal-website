I'm Insaf Ismath — an AI/ML engineer and researcher based in Abu Dhabi. I work at the intersection of
**enterprise AI systems** and **multimodal LLM research**.

## What I do

At 2PointZero (IHC's investment powerhouse), I translate business problems into shippable AI systems.
Recent work includes a geospatial decision platform for a 134-branch UAE salon network, a real-time
LiveKit voice agent for C-suite engagement, and an AutoML benchmarking agent over PostgreSQL/pgvector.

Earlier, I published research on large multimodal models — work that has appeared at **EMNLP** and **CVPR**.

## How I work

I prefer **end-to-end ownership**: scoping the business problem, choosing the right model and data,
shipping the system, and instrumenting it for measurable outcomes. I lean on the boring parts of
software (tests, schemas, observability) so the AI parts can move fast.
