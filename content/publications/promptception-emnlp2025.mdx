## Headline finding

Up to **15 accuracy points** of deviation across an LMM's outputs from prompt
phrasing alone - same model, same questions, different prompt frame. That moves
"which model is best?" from a number into a distribution.

## Why a 61-prompt × 15-category grid

Single-prompt evaluation conflates *capability* with *prompt fit*. A grid of phrasings
grouped into interpretable categories lets the swing be attributed to *which kind* of
prompt change a model is sensitive to - task framing, role priming, output format,
refusal phrasing - instead of producing one collapsed delta.
