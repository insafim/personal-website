## Why skeleton-based classification

Running an RGB-based action classifier per-pair across a crowded frame doesn't fit
the real-time budget. Skeletons are a far smaller representation and — for
two-person interactions — carry most of the discriminative signal, which is what
made the real-time, multi-dyadic setting tractable on commodity hardware.
