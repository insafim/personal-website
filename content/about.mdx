I'm Insaf Ismath - an AI/ML engineer and researcher based in Abu Dhabi. I work where
**business strategy and frontier AI converge**, and I gravitate toward problems where
the technical solution is inseparable from the business context.

## What I do

At 2PointZero - an energy and consumer investment platform under International Holding
Company (IHC) - I take problems end-to-end: scoping with stakeholders, designing the
system, implementing it, and rolling it out. Recent work spans a geospatial decision
platform for a 134-branch UAE salon network, a real-time avatar voice agent for a
C-suite event, a conversational workforce benchmarking agent over hybrid RAG, and a
portfolio-wide rollout of 8,000+ custom GPTs.

Before 2PointZero, I worked on multimodal LLM research at MBZUAI's IVAL, with results
published at **EMNLP 2025 Findings** and **CVPR 2026**, and on synthetic-data
augmentation for face recognition at VisionLabs.

## How I work

I prefer **end-to-end ownership** - scoping the business problem, choosing the right

data-led solutions, and I lean on the boring parts of software (tests, schemas,
observability) so the AI parts can move fast.
